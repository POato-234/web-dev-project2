Where was treaty 1 negotiated?

Treaty 1 was negotiated and signed at lower fort garry in August 1871, between
the Crown and the Anishinaabe and Swampy Cree, canadian based First Nations.
The first of the treaties were called numbered treaties that occured in
beteen 1871 and 1921, it was essentialy held tobe about peace and friendship
but 8 days later the treaty making ended with the indigenous groups agreeing
to give up there land in southern manitoba to the crown in exchange for goods
like clothing and farming tools. but the leaders did not acctualy intend to
give up the land but they believed that the land should be shared no surrenderd.



What makes treaty 1 different?

Treaty 1 is defferent than other treaties in many ways, it was the first formal
